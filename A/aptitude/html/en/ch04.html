This section commemorates some of the people who have contributed to aptitude over its lifetime.

Note

This section is presently rather incomplete and will likely be updated and expanded as time goes on (in particular, there are many missing translation credits due to the huge number of sources of translations [21]). If you think you should be on this list, please email <dburrows@debian.org> with an explanation of why you think so.
